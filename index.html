About me

Turning curiosity
into craft.

I'm a UI/UX design student at Kimpo University with a passion for building products that feel natural and delightful. I recently completed a UI/UX Design Course and apply that knowledge in real-world settings as a QA Intern at Cloud Sathi, where I test and refine the ConnQ mobile application.

My work sits at the intersection of user research, visual design, and interaction craft. I believe good design is invisible — it's the moment a user does exactly what they intended without thinking about it.

When I'm not in Figma, I'm building websites, learning SEO, and contributing to Nepal's growing tech ecosystem.
